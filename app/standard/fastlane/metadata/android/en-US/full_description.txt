<b>Translate You</b> is a supercharged version of <a href="https://apt.izzysoft.de/packages/com.bnyro.translate/">Translate You libre</a> 

Features:
- Translate from or to more than 150 languages
Thanks to the support of numerous translation APIs;
Libre translate, Lingva translate, Deepl and MyMemory engines.
- Designed with Material Design 3 (You)
Beauty, simplicity, functionality in the line of sight.
- Entirely Open Source
Your privacy matters to us, our <a href="https://github.com/Bnyro/TranslateYou">source code</a> is auditable;
our <a href="https://github.com/Bnyro/TranslateYou">privacy policy is clear and accessible.
- Written in Jetpack Compose
Coded with a native and flexible language.
- Support native Android sharing system
Ease of use, seamlessly integrates into Android.
- Multilingual interface
Lovingly translated by our user community.
- Translation History
Save your translations, regardless of the engines used.
- Dark and light theme
To follow your desires, everytimes.

<i>Some translation engines require the use of an API key (free).</i>
